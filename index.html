Hi, my name is Emma Macey and welcome to my portfolio site. On this website you can see my design portfolio highlighting a range of my skills in; print design, logo design, web design & more…


I started out creating fliers at Harbour Youth Service Coffs Harbour in 2003. I have found many opportunities there for design. I was employed by Harbour Youth Service in early 2008, after many years as a volunteer. I continue to find numerous occasions to contribute my design experience to my job as an administrative assistant. I also work for Jetty Bunker Youth Service a few nights a week as a support worker, teaching cooking to residents, assisting with menu plans and shopping and whole range of other tasks.   

 
I am currently available for hire as a graphic designer, specialising in the fields of web design, print and logo design. With the majority of my spare time spent researching various elements of graphic design, as well as designing and implementing promotions for small businesses such as logos, websites, posters, brochures and more…


A large part of my life is dedicated to improving and expanding my techniques. I love design, I am passionate about it, at times I even think about it in my sleep. It has become apart of me and what I do and continues to evolve within me.
